Important Drug Facts

Aspirin

Originally derived from willow bark, aspirin inhibits prostaglandin synthesis and is used as an analgesic, antipyretic, and anti-inflammatory agent.

Penicillin

Discovered by Alexander Fleming in 1928, penicillin was the first antibiotic. It works by inhibiting bacterial cell wall synthesis.

Morphine

An opioid pain medication, morphine acts on the Î¼-opioid receptors in the central nervous system to relieve pain.

Sertraline

An SSRI antidepressant, sertraline increases serotonin levels in the brain and is used to treat depression, anxiety, and OCD.

Ibuprofen

Ibuprofen is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used for reducing pain, fever, and inflammation.

Paracetamol

Paracetamol is an analgesic and antipyretic used to treat pain and fever. It works by inhibiting the synthesis of prostaglandins.

Amoxicillin

Amoxicillin is a broad-spectrum antibiotic used to treat bacterial infections by inhibiting cell wall synthesis.

Metformin

Metformin is an oral antidiabetic drug used to manage type 2 diabetes by decreasing glucose production in the liver.

Atorvastatin

Atorvastatin is a statin used to lower cholesterol levels and reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease.

Warfarin

Warfarin is an anticoagulant that prevents blood clots by inhibiting the synthesis of vitamin K-dependent clotting factors.

Omeprazole

Omeprazole is a proton pump inhibitor (PPI) used to treat gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD) and peptic ulcers.

Lisinopril

Lisinopril is an ACE inhibitor used to treat high blood pressure and heart failure by relaxing blood vessels.

Metoprolol

Metoprolol is a beta-blocker used to treat high blood pressure, angina, and heart rhythm disorders.

Albuterol

Albuterol is a bronchodilator used to treat asthma and other conditions with reversible airway obstruction.

Levothyroxine

Levothyroxine is a synthetic form of thyroid hormone used to treat hypothyroidism.

Hydrochlorothiazide

Hydrochlorothiazide is a diuretic used to treat high blood pressure and edema by removing excess fluid from the body.

Clopidogrel

Clopidogrel is an antiplatelet medication that prevents blood clots by inhibiting platelet aggregation.

Ciprofloxacin

Ciprofloxacin is a broad-spectrum antibiotic used to treat bacterial infections by inhibiting DNA gyrase.

Fluoxetine

Fluoxetine is an SSRI antidepressant used to treat depression, anxiety, and OCD by increasing serotonin levels in the brain.

Losartan

Losartan is an angiotensin II receptor blocker (ARB) used to treat high blood pressure and protect the kidneys in diabetic patients.
